Re-imagining the immediate post graduate experience

Project Description


As part of a class project we were required to collaboratively work with San Josè State University and an Intuit mentor to develop a method to improve the SJSU alumni experience. My group developed an internal online portal called Bridge. This portal allows students, staff and alumni to connect with each other. Bridge gives alumni the ability to start events, volunteer, build professional connections and get more involved with the university.

My Role

I led all of the research efforts as a group member. I created a competitor analysis, regularly met with an Intuit mentor to discuss the project details, generated solution, designed and developed the online beta landing page, and designed the entire landing portal.
