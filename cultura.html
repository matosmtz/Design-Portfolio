Re-imagining the experience of eating out.

Mobile App Concept

Cultura is a mobile application that seeks to ease the restaurant selection process. The app begins by asking the user a set of questions regarding personal food preference. The app then generates restaurant suggestions according to the user’s taste. The application also has a group feature, which allows the user to start a “table” with friends and search for a restaurant that accommodates all members’ preferences. This feature is extremely useful for those with particular food accommodations such as allergies and vegetarianism.

My Role

I was responsible for developing the concept, group feature, business model and one third of the screens for this application.
